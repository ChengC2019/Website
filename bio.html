Cheng's Bio

Postdoctoral Researcher + Course Instructor

Dr. Cheng Chen's CV

Currently, I am a postdoc researcher and part-time instructor at the University of Georgia. My research interest is in developing digital threads to connect requirement management and computer-aided design using machine learning. The increasing use of data-driven approaches in design and manufacturing has led my secondary research area to focus on how AI can be incorporated into STEM education. My master's thesis focused on improving the prediction accuracy of spray droplet size and velocity distribution using the maximum entropy principle (MaxEnt) and MPI.
