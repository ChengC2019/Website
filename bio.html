Dr. Cheng Chen's CV

My name is Cheng Chen, and I am a mechanical engineer currently specializing in developing and implementing digital threads for use in data-driven smart manufacturing. My research focuses on the development of interdomain analyses and interfaces to improve engineering design efficiency and decision making. As a researcher, I am passionate about exploring innovative approaches, collaborating with other domain experts, and developing interdisciplinary research projects.

Academic Background:
